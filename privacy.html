Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Salt & Light Wellness ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our website or services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Booking information: Name, phone number, email address, preferred service, date and time of appointment, and optional notes.
  • Intake form data: Health concerns, pain levels, wellness goals, and consent acknowledgments.
  • Waiver information: Digital signature, date signed, IP address, and waiver version for legal compliance.
  • Testimonials: Name and written testimonial if voluntarily submitted.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To confirm and manage your reservations
  • To send appointment reminders via email
  • To maintain your annual waiver on file
  • To provide personalized wellness services
  • To comply with applicable laws and regulations

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data may be shared only with:

  • Fresha (our scheduling and payment platform) for appointment and payment processing
  • Email service providers for sending confirmations and reminders
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Data Retention

We retain your booking records and intake forms for a minimum of 3 years for business and legal purposes. Signed waivers are retained indefinitely as part of our audit trail. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us directly.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or loss. Our website uses HTTPS encryption for all data transmission.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out of marketing emails at any time

7. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies for essential functionality only (e.g., admin session management). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your personal data, please contact us:

North Conway, NH
Email: info@saltandlightwellness.com

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

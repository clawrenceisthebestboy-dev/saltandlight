✦ Common Questions

What to Expect

Do I need an appointment?

Yes — sessions are by appointment. Book online or call us and we'll get you in fast.

How often should I come?

For best results, most clients do 3–5 sessions per week to start. HaloRed can be used once per day. PEMI up to 20–30 minutes per open day. We'll help you build a plan.

Is it safe?

Yes. Both therapies are non-invasive and drug-free. If you have a specific medical condition, check with your doctor — but the vast majority of people can use these without any issues.

What should I wear?

Comfortable, loose-fitting clothing. For red light therapy, more skin exposure = better results, so light layers are ideal.

Can I combine both therapies in one visit?

Absolutely — many clients do. Ask about our combo packages when you book.
